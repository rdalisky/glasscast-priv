Data Deletion Instructions for GlassCast

Last updated: May 16, 2026

GlassCast respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to remove any data associated with your use of the GlassCast app and its integration with Meta (Facebook) accounts and Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses.

What Data We Store

GlassCast stores minimal data:

  • Your Meta account identifier and authentication token (used only to connect to your wearable device).
  • Media (photos and videos) that you explicitly import — stored locally on your iOS device only.

We do not maintain a server-side database of your personal information.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1 — Delete from the App

  1. Open GlassCast on your iPhone.
  2. Go to Settings inside the app.
  3. Tap Sign Out to disconnect your Meta account and remove the stored token.
  4. Delete the app from your device to remove all locally cached media and data.

Option 2 — Revoke Access from Meta

  1. Open the Facebook app or visit facebook.com/settings?tab=business_tools.
  2. Find GlassCast in the list of connected apps.
  3. Tap Remove to revoke GlassCast's access to your Meta account.

Option 3 — Email Request

To request deletion of any remaining data we may hold related to your account, email us at:
rdalisky@gmail.com

Please include the subject line Data Deletion Request and the email or Meta user ID associated with your account. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.
