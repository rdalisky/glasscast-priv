Privacy Policy for GlassCast

Last updated: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GlassCast ("we," "our," or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application and its integration with Meta wearable devices (Ray-Ban Meta smart glasses and related Meta platform services).

1. Information We Collect

GlassCast may collect the following information when you connect your Meta account:

  • Account information from Meta (name, profile identifier, email) provided through Facebook Login.
  • Media content (photos and videos) that you explicitly choose to import or cast from your Meta wearable device.
  • Device information such as iOS version and device model, used solely for compatibility and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Authenticate you with Meta and enable wearable device integration.
  • Display, cast, and manage your media within the app.
  • Improve app performance and resolve technical issues.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

GlassCast does not sell your personal information. Media and account data remain on your device or within your authenticated Meta session. We do not share your data with third parties except as required to provide the requested functionality (e.g., Meta's official APIs).

4. Data Retention

You may revoke access at any time from your Meta account settings or by signing out of the app. Local data is removed when you delete the app from your device.

5. Children's Privacy

GlassCast is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including OAuth authentication and HTTPS, to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
